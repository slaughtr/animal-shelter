All of our puppies are rescue animals from the National Institutes of Mental Health. All of them have been subjected to cruel experiments, and several of them have super powers.

Take a moment to view the profiles of some of our adoptable puppies and then call us to schedule a visit.

Bolt

Bolt's power is immortality. He came to America on the Mayflower with Miles Standish. The Flash gave him an immortality serum during the Infinity Crisis. He is technically the First Avenger.

Captain Spaghetti

Captain Spaghetti's power is turning Americans into spaghetti, which his buddy Captain America then eats.

Joe

Joe likes naps. Joe's power is the ability to run at the speed of sound for incredibly brief periods of time. Then he must sleep.


Vanessa

Vanessa's super power is the ability to play dead like no other dog on earth. She probably hates you. She hates everyone except for and especially her sister Fluffy. Vanessa is complicated.

Fluffy

Fluffy's super power is love. And the ability to cause inter-cerebral aneurisms with a glance. Because of their close and complicated bond, Fluffy and Vanessa may only be adopted together.
